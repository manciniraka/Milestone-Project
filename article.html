Desa Wisata Bantar Agung

Desa Wisata Bantar Agung, yang berada di Kecamatan Sindangwangi, Kabupaten Majalengka, Jawa Barat, dikenal akan keindahan alam yang masih alami serta suasana pedesaan yang menenangkan. Desa ini menawarkan beragam daya tarik wisata alam, seperti persawahan terasering yang indah, air terjun yang menawan, serta pemandangan megah Gunung Ciremai. Selain itu, pengunjung dapat menikmati kebudayaan lokal yang kaya dengan tradisi dan mencicipi kuliner khas dari Desa Bantar Agung. Dengan keramahan warganya serta keaslian lingkungan pedesaannya, Desa Wisata Bantar Agung menjadi destinasi favorit bagi wisatawan domestik dan internasional yang ingin merasakan ketenangan alam.

Bantar Agung Tourism Village, located in Sindangwangi District, Majalengka Regency, West Java, is known for its natural beauty and calming rural atmosphere. This village offers a variety of natural tourist attractions, such as beautiful terraced rice fields, charming waterfalls, and magnificent views of Mount Ciremai. In addition, visitors can enjoy the local culture rich in tradition and taste the culinary specialties of Bantar Agung Village. With the friendliness of its residents and the authenticity of its rural environment, Bantar Agung Tourism Village is a favorite destination for domestic and international tourists who want to experience the tranquility of nature.
