Deskripsi Aktivitas

Desa Wisata Bantaragung, yang berada di Kabupaten Majalengka, Jawa Barat, terletak di lereng Gunung Ciremai. Desa ini menawarkan pengalaman trekking dan penjelajahan alam yang menyenangkan, sambil menyusuri desa bersama pemandu lokal. Sepanjang perjalanan, pemandu akan berbagi kisah mengenai sejarah dan asal-usul Desa Bantaragung, menambah nilai edukatif pada pengalaman wisata.

~

Bantaragung Tourism Village, located in Majalengka Regency, West Java, is located on the slopes of Mount Ciremai. This village offers a pleasant trekking and nature exploration experience, while exploring the village with a local guide. Along the way, the guide will share stories about the history and origins of Bantaragung Village, adding educational value to the tourism experience.
